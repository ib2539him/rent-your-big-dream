Business Made Easy

By renting a company, you enter into a partnership with Rented Company, in which Rented Company acts as a commission agent that takes care of payments and paperwork so you can focus completely on your work and customers.

You can send invoices from Rented Company, receive the payments from your customers, pay your subcontractors and suppliers and withdraw money to your bank account, PayPal, debit/credit card or e-wallet as compensation or secondary income.
